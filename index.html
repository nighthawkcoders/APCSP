--- layout: home search_exclude: true image: images/logo.png --- ## Objectives

This Course Framework teaches Python, College Boards 5 Big Ideas of AP Computer Science Principles, and a Web Site application development together in an integrated fashion. The purpose is to teach College Board requirements while adding frameworks that meet Career Technical Education (CTE) needs. The outcome is preparing the High School student for the AP Test and simultaneously building the skills required for Computer Science internships.
